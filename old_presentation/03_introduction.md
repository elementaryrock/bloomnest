# Introduction

---

## The Vision Behind NeuroNest

> **"Every child deserves tools that understand them. Every family deserves support that sees the whole picture."**

NeuroNest is more than software. It's a **comprehensive therapeutic ecosystem** designed to support children with special needs and the families who love them.

---

## The Challenge We're Addressing

### Children with Developmental Challenges

Millions of children worldwide face developmental challenges that require consistent therapeutic intervention:

| Condition | Full Form | Impact |
|-----------|-----------|--------|
| **ASD** | Autism Spectrum Disorder | Affects communication, behavior, social interaction |
| **SLD** | Specific Learning Disability | Affects reading, writing, mathematical skills |
| **ID** | Intellectual Disability | Limits intellectual functioning and adaptive behavior |
| **CP** | Cerebral Palsy | Affects movement, muscle coordination, posture |
