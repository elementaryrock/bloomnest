## Why We Chose This Problem

### 1. Social Impact

> **"Every child deserves access to quality therapy services without the barriers of inefficient systems."**

```
┌─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┐
│               CHILDREN WITH SPECIAL NEEDS                   │
├─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┤
│                                                             │
│   • 1 in 100 children diagnosed with ASD globally          │
│   • 5-15% of children have learning disabilities           │
│   • Early intervention significantly improves outcomes     │
│   • Regular therapy sessions are crucial for development   │
│                                                             │
└─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┘
```

---

### 2. Real-World Problem

- Observed **actual challenges** at therapy centers
- Parents struggle with booking and tracking
- Therapists overwhelmed with paperwork
- Administrative inefficiencies affect service quality

---

### 3. Technology as an Enabler

```
    TRADITIONAL APPROACH              DIGITAL SOLUTION
    ─────────────────────             ─────────────────

    📞 Phone calls                    🌐 Online booking
    📝 Paper records                  💾 Digital database
    🗂️ File cabinets                  🔍 Instant search
    ⏰ Limited hours                  ⏰ 24/7 access
    ❌ No analytics                   📊 Real-time insights
```
