## Future Vision - Phase 2 Features

### 🌊 Therapy Ripple - Family Wellbeing Ecosystem

> Track the therapy's impact on the ENTIRE family, not just the child.

- Weekly parent stress/mood tracking
- Optional sibling mood check-ins
- Correlation analysis between child progress and family wellbeing
- Visual "ripple effect" dashboard

### 🎨 StoryBuilder - AI-Powered Social Stories

> "See it to believe it." Personalized visual stories where the child is the hero.

- Upload child's photo → AI generates personalized story images
- Pre-built scenarios (dentist, haircut, school, etc.)
- Child's favorite comfort object included
- Export as PDF for offline use

### 🌱 SkillSprout - Gamified Progress Garden

> Watch skills grow like plants in a virtual garden.

- Each therapy skill = a plant species
- Daily "watering" ritual builds habits
- Plants grow as children practice skills
- Therapists can gift "rare seeds" for achievements
- Milestone celebrations with animations
