## The Statistics That Moved Us

| Fact | Implication |
|------|-------------|
| **1 in 100** children diagnosed with ASD globally | Millions of families need support |
| **5-15%** of children have learning disabilities | Therapy demand is enormous |
| **70%** of parents report high stress levels | Family wellbeing often ignored |
| **60%** of children with ASD have dental anxiety | Preparation tools urgently needed |
| Early intervention improves outcomes by **50%+** | Consistent therapy is crucial |
