## The Two Phases

### Phase 1: Foundation (Current - Implemented)
- Multi-role authentication system
- Patient registration & management
- Session booking & scheduling
- Therapist workflow & documentation
- Administrative dashboard

### Phase 2: Innovation (Future - Planned)
- 🌊 **Therapy Ripple** - Family wellbeing tracking
- 🎨 **StoryBuilder** - AI-powered social stories
- 🌱 **SkillSprout** - Gamified progress garden

- **Project:** NeuroNest - Therapeutic Support Platform
