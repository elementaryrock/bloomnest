# Slide 10: PILLAR III — THERAPYRIPPLE™
## The Family Ecosystem

### The Philosophy:
- Therapy doesn't exist in a vacuum. A child's growth is directly tied to the stability of the home environment.

### Core Features:
- **Ripple Analysis:** Correlating clinical sessions with family stress levels and sibling emotional states.
- **Biometric Sentinels:** Parents log weekly "Family Vibes" to identify hidden roadblocks to progress.
- **Visual Trends:** sparkline trends and animated ripple visualizations to measure holistic well-being.

---
*The Goal: Building a support system that balances the pressure of clinical interventions.*
