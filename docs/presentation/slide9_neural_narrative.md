# Slide 9: PILLAR II — NEURALNARRATIVE™
## The Generative Brain

### The Challenge:
- Conventional social scripts are static and unengaging for neurodivergent children.

### The Solution:
- **Generative Empathy:** Using LLMs to craft stories where the child's own identity and challenges are the core of the plot.
- **Multimodal AI:** 
  - **Gemini 2.5 Flash:** Handles the nuanced, scientifically-aligned logic of behavioral scripts.
  - **Imagen 4:** Generates consistent, non-threatening, and beautiful illustrations.

### Key Workflows:
- Therapist sets the "Scenario" (e.g., Dentist Visit).
- AI generates a hero-arc story featuring the child.
- Parent reads the personalized book with the child at home.
