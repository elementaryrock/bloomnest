# Slide 8: PILLAR I — SKILLSPROUT
## The Interaction Engine

### Concept:
- **Bio-Digital Feedback:** Every clinical goal is a seed; every achievement is a flourishing forest.
- **Micro-Milestones:** Breaking down massive developmental targets into small, achievable "watering" tasks.

### The Gamification Loop:
- **XP Ecosystem:** Points awarded for therapy attendance and goal completion.
- **Visual Evolution:** Interactive 3D garden that transforms from a barren plot to a vibrant forest.
- **Engagement:** Watering streaks and badge unlocks to drive daily participation.

---
*Direct Impact: Turning clinical compliance into a voluntary, joyful ritual for the child.*
